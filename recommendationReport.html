Recommendation Report

Recommendation #1 - Iris

Iris recognition technology is the highest recommended biometric technology
for your facility due to the required protection of safety critical assets, 
the ability to read the biometric while the individual is at rest, and the 
primary usage of the biometric technology being indoors.

Recommendation #2 - Facial

Facial recognition technology is the second highest recommended biometric 
technology for your facility due to the controlled environment (indoor and 
individual at rest) mitigating many concerns around facial recognition. The 
probability of required personal protective equipment, such as hard hats, 
ear protection, and safety glasses lowered the recommendation score for 
facial recognition at your facility.

Recommendation #3 - Voice

Voice recognition technology is the third highest recommended biometric 
technology as there is low levels of ambient noise and little impact with 
personal protective equipment. The possibility of using a biometric tech-
nology without a second authentication factor significantly lowered the 
recommendation score for voice recognition at your facility.
